<h1>🎙️ AI Meeting Notes Assistant</h1>

<p>
An AI-powered application that converts meeting audio into text, generates intelligent meeting summaries, and enables users to ask questions about past meetings using Generative AI.
</p>

<p>
Built with <strong>Python</strong>, <strong>Google Gemini AI</strong>, <strong>LangChain</strong>, <strong>Speech-to-Text Processing</strong>, <strong>Vector Search</strong>, and <strong>Streamlit</strong>, this project helps teams automatically document meetings and retrieve insights from previous discussions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🚀 Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>✅ Upload meeting audio files (.MP3, .WAV, .M4A)</li>
  <li>✅ Speech-to-Text transcription (Audio → Text)</li>
  <li>✅ Automatic meeting transcript generation</li>
  <li>✅ AI-powered meeting summarization</li>
  <li>✅ Generate professional meeting notes automatically</li>
  <li>✅ Remove unnecessary filler words and noise</li>
  <li>✅ Extract key discussion points and action items</li>
  <li>✅ Ask questions about past meetings (Meeting Q&A)</li>
  <li>✅ Intelligent retrieval of previous meeting context</li>
  <li>✅ Streamlit-based interactive UI</li>
  <li>✅ Gemini AI integration for smart responses</li>
  <li>✅ Modular and scalable architecture</li>
</ul>

<h2>💡 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
The <strong>AI Meeting Notes Assistant</strong> automatically converts meeting audio into structured notes and enables intelligent interaction with past meeting discussions.
</p>

<p>
Instead of manually reviewing long meeting recordings, users can:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>📂 Upload meeting audio</li>
  <li>📝 Automatically generate transcripts</li>
  <li>🤖 Get AI-powered summaries</li>
  <li>✅ Extract key decisions and action items</li>
  <li>💬 Ask questions from previous meetings</li>
</ul>

<h3>Example Questions</h3>

<ul>
  <li>“What was discussed about deployment?”</li>
  <li>“Who was assigned frontend work?”</li>
  <li>“What were the action items from last week?”</li>
  <li>“When is the delivery deadline?”</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>⚙️ How It Works</h2>

<h3>Step 1 → Upload Meeting Audio</h3>

<p><strong>Supported Formats:</strong></p>

<ul>
  <li>MP3</li>
  <li>WAV</li>
  <li>M4A</li>
</ul>

<p>⬇️</p>

<h3>Step 2 → Speech-to-Text Processing</h3>

<p>
The model converts speech into clean text transcripts.
</p>

<p>⬇️</p>

<h3>Step 3 → Transcript Cleaning</h3>

<p>Removes:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Filler words</li>
  <li>Repetitions</li>
  <li>Irrelevant conversations</li>
</ul>

<p>⬇️</p>

<h3>Step 4 → AI Summarization</h3>

<p>Generates:</p>

<ul>
  <li>📌 Key Discussion Points</li>
  <li>📝 Meeting Summary</li>
  <li>✅ Action Items</li>
  <li>🎯 Important Decisions</li>
</ul>

<p>⬇️</p>

<h3>Step 5 → Meeting Q&A (RAG)</h3>

<p>
Users can ask questions about previous meetings and get context-aware answers powered by AI.
</p>
